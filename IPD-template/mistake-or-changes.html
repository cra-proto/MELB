If there is a mistake or something changes

Life changes. This section tells users what to do if their situation changes after they have already submitted, or if they realize they made an error.

On this page

  • What to include
  • Guidance
  • Recommended headings

What to include

  • How to correct an error on a submission that has already been sent (amend a return, resubmit a form, call to update)
  • How to report a change in circumstances (change of address, marital status, income, dependents)
  • Deadlines for making corrections
  • Whether changes trigger a reassessment or recalculation
  • How to update personal information on file

Guidance

  • Separate errors from life changes; they often have different processes
  • Link to the relevant amendment or update tool (for example, “Change my return” in My Account, the T1-ADJ form)
  • If the user needs to notify the CRA proactively (for example, change of address), make it clear this is their responsibility and link to the process

Recommended headings

  • If you made a mistake
  • If your situation changes
  • How to make a correction
  • Update your information
