If you do not get a response

This section prevents unnecessary phone calls while also giving users a clear path when something has genuinely gone wrong.

On this page

  • What to include
  • Guidance
  • Recommended headings

What to include

  • How long to wait before taking action (tied to the processing time section)
  • What to check first (correct address on file, My Account messages, spam folder if email)
  • A specific action to take if the expected timeframe has passed (call a specific phone line, submit a service complaint, etc.)

Guidance

  • Frame this as a practical checklist, not a list of things that might have gone wrong
  • Link directly to the relevant contact method rather than a generic “Contact us” page
  • Include the phone number and hours of service inline if possible

Recommended headings

  • If you have not received a response
  • What to do if you do not hear from us
