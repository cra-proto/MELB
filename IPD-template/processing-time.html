Processing time

Users need to know how long to wait. This section manages expectations and reduces unnecessary follow-up calls.

On this page

  • What to include
  • Guidance
  • Recommended headings

What to include

  • The standard processing time (for example, “8 weeks from the date we receive your application”)
  • Whether processing time starts from the date of submission, the date of receipt, or some other trigger
  • Factors that may cause delays (incomplete applications, high volume periods, additional review)
  • The difference between electronic and paper submission times, if applicable

Guidance

  • Be as specific as possible — “6 to 8 weeks” is better than “several weeks”
  • If processing times vary by season (for example, tax season vs. off-season), say so
  • State the start point: “8 weeks from the date we receive your return” not just “8 weeks”
  • If you cannot provide a specific time, explain why and tell the user how to check their status instead
  • Update processing times when they change; outdated times erode trust

Recommended headings

  • Processing time
  • How long it takes
  • When to expect a response
