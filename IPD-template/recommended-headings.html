Recommended heading patterns

These heading patterns are drawn from CRA’s existing subway pattern guidance and live service pages. Using consistent headings across services helps users recognize patterns and find information faster.

Recommended heading patterns by content area
Content area Recommended headings
EligibilityWho can apply, Who can claim, Who is eligible, Who must register, Find out if you are eligible
PreparationBefore you start, What you need, Get ready to apply, Get ready to claim, How much you can get
Core actionHow to apply, How to claim, How to file, How to register

ReviewWhat happens next, Our review and decision
