If something goes wrong

This section covers error recovery: what happens if the submission is rejected, denied, or needs correction.

On this page

  • What to include
  • Guidance
  • Recommended headings

What to include

  • Common reasons for rejection or denial
  • How the user will be informed of a problem
  • What the user can do in response (resubmit, appeal, request a review, make a correction)
  • Deadlines for appeals or objections, if applicable
  • Links to the relevant correction or appeal process

Guidance

  • Use plain, non-alarming language — “If your application is not approved” is better than “If your application is denied”
  • Be specific about remedies — “You can request a formal review by writing to...” is better than “You have the right to object”
  • If the correction process is a separate service with its own page, link to it rather than duplicating the content

Recommended headings

  • If your application is not approved
  • If your claim is denied
  • How to request a review
