Title

The title is the single most important piece of content on the page. It sets the user’s expectation for everything that follows.

On this page

  • What to include
  • Guidance

What to include

  • A clear, specific description of the task or service
  • Action-oriented language when the page is task-based

Guidance

  • Use the pattern [Action verb] + [the thing]: Apply for the Canada Dental Benefit, Register for a Business Number, Claim home office expenses
  • Keep it short enough to work in navigation, breadcrumbs, and search results
  • Avoid vague titles like “Information about...” or “Overview of...”
  • The title must match what appears in the navigation and any links pointing to this page
  • Use sentence case (capitalize only the first word and proper nouns)

Examples

Good title examples
  • Apply for the Canada Dental Benefit
  • Register for a Business Number
  • Claim home office expenses
  • File your income tax return
Titles to avoid
  • Information about the Canada Dental Benefit
  • Overview of business registration
  • Home office expenses
  • T1 Individual Income Tax and Benefit Return Filing
