What happens next

This section explains the CRA’s side of the process. The user has done their part; now they need to understand what is happening behind the scenes.

On this page

  • What to include
  • Guidance
  • Recommended headings

What to include

  • What the CRA will do with the submission (review, assess, process, verify)
  • Whether additional information may be requested
  • Whether the submission triggers any other process the user should be aware of
  • Any review or decision stages

Guidance

  • Write from the user’s perspective, not the CRA’s internal process perspective
  • “We will review your application” is better than “The application enters the assessment queue”
  • Be honest about what happens — if there is a manual review, say so
  • If the CRA may contact the user for more information, explain what that contact will look like (letter, phone call, My Account message)

Recommended headings

  • What happens next
  • Our review and decision
  • How your application is processed
