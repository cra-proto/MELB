Income statistics and GST/HST statistics

Use these statistical tables for research, policy, journalism, or data analysis. They show how Canadians file taxes, claim credits, and pay GST/HST, broken down by geography, income range, demographic group, return type, and benefit program.

The data comes from aggregated and anonymized tax returns processed by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). Each report is published as downloadable tables in PDF and CSV (raw data) formats.

Find the right report for your task

The list below maps common research tasks to the right report. Each section further down the page lists every available report in that category.

  • Tax filer data by city, region, or postal area (Census Metropolitan Area, Census Agglomeration, or Forward Sortation Area)
    Use Individual Tax Statistics by Area (ITSA) or Individual Tax Statistics by Forward Sortation Area (FSA)
  • Tax filer data by federal riding
    Use Federal Electoral District Statistics (FEDS)
  • Tax filer data by income range or tax bracket
    Use Individual Tax Statistics by Tax Bracket (ITSTB)
  • Benefit and credit data (Canada Child Benefit, Canada Workers Benefit, Disability Tax Credit, GST/HST credit, Canada Carbon Rebate)
    Use the Benefit and credit statistics section
  • Corporate or trust tax data
    Use the Corporate statistical reports or Trust statistical reports section
  • GST/HST registrant or excise tax data
    Use the GST/HST statistical reports or Excise tax and duty statistics section

Individual statistical reports

  • Federal Electoral District Statistics (FEDS)
    Individual income tax data by federal riding, province, territory, and all of Canada
  • Individual Statistics by Tax Filing Method (ISTFM)
    Statistics on how tax filers filed (paper, NETFILE, EFILE), grouped by demographic and economic characteristics
  • Individual Tax Statistics by Area (ITSA)
    Personal income tax data by geographic area, including Forward Sortation Area (FSA), census division, census subdivision, Census Metropolitan Area (CMA), and Census Agglomeration (CA)
  • Individual Tax Statistics by Forward Sortation Area (FSA)
    Personal income tax data by Forward Sortation Area (the first 3 characters of a postal code)
  • Individual Tax Statistics by Tax Bracket (ITSTB)
    Tax data by federal tax bracket, broken out by province, territory, and all of Canada
  • Selected T1 Statistics
    Statistics based on the most recently assessed individual income tax and benefit returns
  • Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) Statistics
    Key TFSA information for a given tax year, including contributions, withdrawals, and holders by age and income
  • Individual Income Tax Return Statistics (formerly T1 Final Statistics)
    Statistics from approximately 100% of all income tax and benefit returns, including reassessments
  • T1 Filing Compliance
    Statistics on individual returns filed late or on time for a given tax year
  • T1 Preliminary Statistics
    Preliminary data from approximately 95% of income tax and benefit returns (reassessments excluded)

Benefit and credit statistics

  • Canada Child Benefit (CCB) Statistics
    CCB payments for a given benefit year
  • Canada Workers Benefit (CWB) Statistics
    CWB amounts assessed for a given tax year
  • Climate Action Incentive (CAI) Statistics
    CAI claims by tax year
  • Canada Carbon Rebate (CCR) Statistics
    CCR claims by tax year
  • Disability Tax Credit (DTC) Statistics
    DTC information for a given calendar year, including determinations and claims by age, gender, and province
  • GST/HST Credit Statistics
    GST/HST credit payments for a given benefit year
  • Working Income Tax Benefit (WITB) Statistics
    WITB amounts assessed for a given tax year (WITB was replaced by the Canada Workers Benefit in 2019)

Corporate statistical reports

  • T2 Corporate Statistics
    Taxation and selected accounting information for all corporation tax returns that have been assessed or reassessed
  • T2 Corporate Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED) Statistics
    SR&ED tax and accounting information from assessed or reassessed T2 returns

Trust statistical reports

  • Trust Statistics
    Taxation and selected accounting information from all assessed or reassessed trust tax returns

Excise tax and duty statistics

  • Excise Tax
    Excise taxes assessed for a given fiscal year
  • Excise Duty
    Excise duties assessed for a given fiscal year

GST/HST statistical reports

  • GST/HST Incremental Federal Rebate for Municipalities
    The incremental value (42.86%) of the 100% GST rebate, and the federal portion of the HST rebate, approved for payment to each municipal entity
  • GST/HST Statistics
    Detailed profiles of GST/HST registrants

Specialty and historical reports

  • Statistical report on the participation of hard-to-reach populations in the tax and benefit systems
    Tax filing and benefit participation rates of hard-to-reach populations, sometimes referred to as vulnerable populations, built by linking 2021 Census and CRA tax data
  • Foreign Income Verification Statement and Related Statistics Report
    Foreign income verification statement and related statistics
  • Information Returns of Non-Arm's Length Transactions with Non-Residents
    Statistics on non-arm's length transactions between persons resident in Canada and non-resident persons, as reported on form T106
  • Security Options Deductions, Tax Years 2007 to 2017
    Statistics on security options deductions reported on the T1 General for the 2007 to 2017 tax years

Departmental Results Framework

  • Departmental Results Framework (DRF) Indicators
    Indicators that inform parliamentarians and Canadians of the results achieved by government organizations for Canadians
