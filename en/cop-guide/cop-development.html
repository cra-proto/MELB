How a COP is developed

Understand the complete lifecycle that every Content Optimization Project follows, from initial scoping through validation testing and production launch.

This guide was reverse-engineered from 8 actual COPs built by experienced CRA content designers. While each project had its own scope and team, they all followed the same fundamental pattern.
